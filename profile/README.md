## About Us

Nexora Robotics is an open-source robotics engineering initiative focused on designing intelligent robotic systems, embedded platforms, autonomous machines, and artificial intelligence applications.

Our mission is to develop practical engineering projects that are educational, reproducible, and useful to the robotics community.

We believe that knowledge grows when it is shared.
